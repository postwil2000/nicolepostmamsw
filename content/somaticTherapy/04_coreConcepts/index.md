**Somatic Experiencing (SE)**<br>
Developed by Dr. Peter Levine, this technique involves tracking physical sensations (such as a racing heart or shallow breathing) while discussing past stressors to help the nervous system process and release trauma.


**Body Awareness & Scanning**<br>
Mentally scanning the body to identify tension points, numbness or pain, enabling you to recognize your body’s physical cues of stress.


**Titration & Pendulation**<br>
Gently guiding you through a difficult memory in small doses (titration) while moving your focus back and forth between a stressed and relaxed state (pendulation).


**Resourcing**<br>
Identifying specific memories, people or places that make you feel safe, utilising them to anchor yourself when processing difficult emotions. 


These techniques, which highlight how we sense our bodies internally and externally, may help alleviate the effects of trauma, anxiety and pain.
